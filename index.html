Facilities & Services – Vrindavan Diagnostic Services

Committed to accurate, timely, and patient-centered diagnostics powered by advanced technology, expert pathologists, and compassionate care.

1. Clinical Laboratory Diagnostics

We offer a complete range of laboratory investigations with high precision and reliability:

  • Hematology: Complete Blood Count (CBC), Platelet Count, Hemoglobin, ESR, Coagulation Profile, Anemia Workup, Sickle Cell Testing, Blood Indices
  • Biochemistry: Liver Function Tests, Kidney Function Tests, Blood Glucose, Lipid Profile, Electrolytes, Thyroid Hormones, Hormonal Panels, Vitamin & Mineral Assessment
  • Microbiology & Infectious Diseases: Culture & Sensitivity, Blood/Urine/Stool/Sputum Analysis, Bacterial/Viral/Fungal Infection Detection
  • Immunoassays & Serology: Autoimmune markers, Tumor markers, Viral serology (HIV, Hepatitis B/C, Dengue), Allergy & Immune function tests
  • Cytology & Histopathology: FNAC, Tissue Biopsy, Tumor Grading, Cancer Screening, Pap Smear Analysis, Histological Staining
  • Bone Marrow Examination: Aspiration & Biopsy for hematological disorders, leukemia, anemia, blood dyscrasias
  • Specialized Panels: Endocrine, Metabolic, Liver, Renal, and Infectious Disease Panels

2. Preventive Health & Wellness Programs

  • Full body health check-ups for men, women, and senior citizens
  • Corporate wellness programs tailored to employee needs
  • Diabetes, Hypertension, Thyroid, and Cardiac risk screening
  • Metabolic and hormonal assessment
  • Lifestyle and nutrition guidance
  • Periodic health screening packages for early detection

3. Industrial & Occupational Health Services

  • Pre-employment and routine health check-ups
  • Occupational exposure assessments
  • Fitness-for-duty evaluations and medical clearance
  • Workplace wellness initiatives and health education

4. Advanced Diagnostic Technology & Infrastructure

State-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and automated systems ensure reliable and fast results:

  • Automated Hematology, Biochemistry, and Immunoassay analyzers
  • Advanced microscopy for Cytology and Histopathology
  • Digital Laboratory Management System with online report access
  • Stringent quality control protocols and SOPs
  • Data security and confidentiality compliance

5. Patient-Centric Services

  • Fast and accurate reporting
  • Home sample collection and doorstep testing
  • Digital report delivery via email, WhatsApp, and online portal
  • Personalized consultation for abnormal results and follow-up
  • Friendly and trained staff support throughout the process

6. Community Health & Social Responsibility

  • Preventive health screening camps for lifestyle, diabetes, and hypertension
  • Public education on hygiene, wellness, and preventive care
  • Collaboration with community organizations, Rotary Clubs, and social initiatives

7. Accreditation, Quality, and Reliability

  • Strict adherence to national and international laboratory quality standards
  • Experienced and qualified pathologists overseeing all services
  • Ethical, transparent, and patient-first practices
  • Commitment to timely, precise, and trustworthy reporting
